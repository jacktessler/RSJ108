How To Strap Into Your Snowboard

Assembly time: 5-10 Minutes
Awesome rating: 100% Awesome

If you are learning to snowboard this is the best tutorial for setting up your bindings and strapping in! Just follow the instructions and video and you'll be riding in no time!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Snowboard
  • A set of bindings
  • Screws that came with your bindings
  • Screwdriver
  • Washers
  • Snowboarding boots

  1. Consider which foot you will use for your front foot, this should be your dominate foot.

    You can figure out which one is dominate by remembering what foot you have forwards when skating or kicking a ball with.

  2. Place the bindings in the preferred position for you feet on the snowboard

    The bindings should be about shoulder length apart or what position feels the most comfortable to stand on the board in.

    The snowboard should have the bindings uptop as seen in the photo above.

  3. Screw the bindings into place using the screwdriver.

    Make sure you use the washers to keep the screws secure in the board and use the video below to help understand foot position.

  4. With the bindings secured check comfortability by putting your boots on and strapping in.

    Play around with the right placement of your bindings until you get one that works best for you.

  5. You're ready to ride!.

    Now strap your boots into place and get ready to hit the mountain. Don't forget a helmet!.
